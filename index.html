Advance your career

Being a member of IEEE can advance your career in ways you might have not considered before. Regardless of your discipline or field - it pays to have access to the career resources we can provide.

We represent you, the students and faculty, in SICCS and CEIAS. One of our most important tasks is to help smoothen the transition to university for our incoming first year students with a variety of activities; helping to foster friendship, teamwork, and unity in our faculty and develop a feeling of pride for being a student in engineering. We really hope that you come out and get involved in the institue! Feel free to give us a visit at our office during the regular school year in the engineering building, room 237.
